## **2. Description**  
CHS‑OL v1.0 defines the **five‑phase operational lifecycle** for the Cognitive Hypercube System (CHS).

- interprets dimensional input

- maintains continuity across turns  

CHS‑OL is the lifecycle backbone of CHS and is required for all cognitive traversal within UMA.
