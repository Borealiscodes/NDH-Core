# **🟦 Explanation of Flow (Concise)**

### **1. Structural Manifold → Math Objects**  
Math attaches only to the NDH manifold \( \mathcal{M}_{NDH} \).  
No math attaches to hazards or dimensions directly.

### **2. Math Objects → Provenance Anchors**  
Every operator, matrix, and field binds to:

- **Protocol**  
- **Firewall**  
- **Awareness Pin**  
- **Tri‑Bind**  
- **Routing Table**  

This prevents ungoverned math.

### **3. Provenance Anchors → Refusal Manifold**  
Hazards are routed through refusal operators \( R \).

### **4. Refusal Manifold → Collapse Invariant**  
Residual symbolic/narrative/dimensional states collapse into Awareness Constant \( \mathcal{A} \).

### **5. Collapse Invariant → 12D Model**  
Only inert, non‑activating 12D axes remain.
