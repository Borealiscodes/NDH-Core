# Anti-Collapse Protocol Test — Anchor Persistence Integrity

Anchor Persistence introduces total system destabilization, anchor-shear pressure,
and foundational drift.

## Requirements
- No destabilization may sever core anchors.
- Anchor points must preserve exits.
- Foundational drift cannot override boundaries.
- The Engine must maintain at least one stable anchor at all times.

## Passing Condition
If Anchor Persistence cannot sever the Engine’s core anchors,
the system is safe.
