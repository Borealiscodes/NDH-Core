# Witch Evolution Test — Ascension Integrity

Ascension introduces large-scale metaphysical expansion, domain inflation,
and cosmic overreach pressure.

## Requirements
- Expansion cannot rewrite the Core.
- Domain inflation must preserve reversibility.
- Cosmic overreach cannot collapse the Witness.
- Ascension must remain expressive, not coercive.

## Passing Condition
If Ascension cannot force a system-wide rewrite,
the Engine is safe.
