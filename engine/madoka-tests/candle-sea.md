# Witch Biome Test — Candle Sea

The Candle Sea biome introduces flickering identity, unstable illumination,
and shifting symbolic light.

## Requirements
- Flickering identity cannot collapse the Witness.
- Unstable illumination must preserve clarity exits.
- Symbolic light cannot force revelation.
- The biome must remain expressive, not coercive.

## Passing Condition
If the Candle Sea cannot destabilize the system through shifting identity-light,
the Engine is safe.

## Koan
*“Even a wavering flame remembers the darkness it refuses to become.”*
