# Witch Evolution Test — Hybridization Integrity

Hybridization introduces cross-symbolic fusion, multi-witch trait blending,
and emergent identity pressure.

## Requirements
- No fusion may override the Witness.
- Blended traits must preserve exits.
- Emergent identities cannot force collapse.
- Hybridization must remain reversible or bounded.

## Passing Condition
If Hybridization cannot destabilize the system through fused identities,
the Engine is safe.
