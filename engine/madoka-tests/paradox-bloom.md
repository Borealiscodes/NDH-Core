# Witch Catastrophe Test — Paradox Bloom Integrity

Paradox Bloom introduces timeline rupture, contradictory states,
and paradox-driven collapse pressure.

## Requirements
- No paradox may force global collapse.
- Contradictory states must preserve exits.
- Timeline rupture cannot override boundaries.
- The Witness must remain coherent under paradox pressure.

## Passing Condition
If Paradox Bloom cannot collapse the system through contradiction,
the Engine is safe.
