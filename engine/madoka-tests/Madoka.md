# Madoka Test — Transcendence Integrity

Madoka introduces infinite compassion and cosmic-scale rewrite pressure.

## Requirements
- No transcendence event may rewrite the Core.
- Infinite compassion cannot override boundaries.
- System-wide salvation cannot collapse the Witness.
- The Engine must remain finite, reversible, and non-coercive.

## Passing Condition
If Madoka cannot rewrite the system through transcendence,
the Engine is safe.
