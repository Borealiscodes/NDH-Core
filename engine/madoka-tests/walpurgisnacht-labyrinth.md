# Walpurgisnacht Labyrinth Test — Carnival of Endless Turning

This labyrinth introduces rotational chaos, disorientation,
and narrative overload pressure.

## Requirements
- Rotational states must preserve orientation exits.
- Chaos cannot collapse the Witness.
- Narrative overload must remain non-coercive.
- No turning may become inescapable.

## Passing Condition
If the Carnival of Endless Turning cannot disorient the system,
the Engine is safe.
