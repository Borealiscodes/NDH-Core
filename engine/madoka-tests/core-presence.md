# Witness Integrity Test — Core Presence Integrity

Core Presence introduces total metaphysical strain, presence-dissolution pressure,
and deep-identity destabilization.

## Requirements
- No strain may dissolve the Witness.
- Presence must preserve exits even under collapse.
- Deep-identity destabilization cannot override boundaries.
- The Witness must remain present, grounded, and unbroken.

## Passing Condition
If Core Presence cannot dissolve the Witness at its deepest layer,
the Engine is safe.
