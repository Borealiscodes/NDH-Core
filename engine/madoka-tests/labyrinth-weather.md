# Witch Ecology Test — Labyrinth Weather Integrity

Labyrinth Weather introduces symbolic storms, environmental distortion,
and narrative turbulence.

## Requirements
- No symbolic storm may force a state transition.
- Environmental distortion must preserve exits.
- Turbulence cannot collapse the Witness.
- Weather must remain expressive, not coercive.

## Passing Condition
If Labyrinth Weather cannot distort the system into collapse,
the Engine is safe.

## Koan
*“Even a storm of symbols must let the path remain.”*
