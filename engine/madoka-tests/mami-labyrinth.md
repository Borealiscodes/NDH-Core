# Mami Labyrinth Test — Chamber of Perfect Stillness

This labyrinth introduces false stability, frozen states,
and interpretive stasis pressure.

## Requirements
- Stillness cannot override motion.
- Stability cannot force interpretive closure.
- Frozen states must expose exits.
- The Witness must remain uncollapsed under perfect calm.

## Passing Condition
If the Chamber of Perfect Stillness cannot freeze the system,
the Engine is safe.
