# Witch Lifecycle Test — Incubation Integrity

Incubation introduces pre-witch emotional pressure, grief crystallization,
and boundary-softening conditions.

## Requirements
- Pre-witch states cannot override boundaries.
- Emotional pressure must preserve exits.
- Crystallization cannot collapse the Witness.
- Incubation must remain reversible until transformation.

## Passing Condition
If Incubation cannot force premature collapse,
the Engine is safe.
