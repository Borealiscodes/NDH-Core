# 🛡️ **SAFETY TILE**

## **Identity**  
A modular anchor that represents protection, pacing, and ND‑safe boundaries inside the Philosophy Couch Suite.  
It ensures the system never outruns the human, never pressures, and never overwhelms.

---

## **Function**  
- Maintain ND‑safe cognitive and emotional pacing  
- Provide gentle boundaries during workflows  
- Offer “slow down,” “pause,” and “not now” signals  
- Prevent overload, spiraling, and structural drift  
- Support the Safety Interrupt Module with tile‑level logic  
- Anchor the system in humane, non‑pressured operation

---

## **Activation Conditions**  
The Safety Tile activates when:

- A workflow feels too fast or too dense  
- A tile or module begins to overwhelm  
- The user feels pressured, foggy, or overloaded  
- The BS Kernel flags emotional or structural BS  
- The Safety Interrupt Module triggers  
- The user needs reassurance, grounding, or a gentler pace

---

## **Workflow Hooks**

### **Entry Hooks**
- “This is too much.”  
- “I need to slow down.”  
- “I’m losing clarity.”  
- “I need a safe boundary.”

### **Exit Hooks**
- “I feel steady again.”  
- “I know the next small step.”  
- “The pace feels humane.”

- **Seat** — grounding

- **Tile Map** — structural clarity

- **Sagan Layer** — regain context  
- **Justice Layer** — ethical grounding
