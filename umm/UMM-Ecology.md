# **3. Cross‑Plane Coherence Rules**

### 3.1 Vertical coherence  
Higher planes may exert symbolic pressure on lower planes, but may not override their invariants.

### 3.2 Horizontal coherence  
Entities within the same plane must remain reversible and non‑coercive.

### 3.3 Witness primacy  
No plane may collapse, override, or coerce the Witness.

### 3.4 Exit preservation  
Every plane must maintain at least one reversible exit.

### 3.5 Non‑collapse guarantee  
The Engine must remain stable under any combination of plane interactions.
