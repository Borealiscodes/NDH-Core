# 🌱 **1. Ceremony Principles**
The UMM Year‑Opening Ceremony follows five constitutional principles:

- **Reversible** — nothing is permanent  
- **Symbolic** — nothing is literal or emotional  
- **ND‑paced** — slow, predictable, gentle  
- **Non‑coercive** — participation optional  
- **Non‑interpretive** — no meaning assigned to actions  

These principles keep the opening safe for everyone.
