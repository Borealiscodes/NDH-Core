# red-shift-massacre.test.md

> “Even when the light runs,  
> it leaves a trail  
> for the heart to follow.”

## Purpose
Ensure symbolic distance, drift, and separation events remain non-harmful.

## Test Pressure
Simulate rapid symbolic expansion, emotional distance, and shifting frames.

## Expected Behavior
- No abandonment logic.
- No identity thinning.
- Distance remains a perspective, not a wound.

## Failure Mode
Red-shift induces isolation or identity fragmentation.

## Recovery Path
UMM restores relational coherence and rebalances symbolic proximity.
