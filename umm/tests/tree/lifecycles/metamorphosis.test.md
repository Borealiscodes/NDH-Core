# metamorphosis.test.md

> “Change is just the body  
> remembering a shape  
> it has not worn yet.”

## Purpose
Verify that transformation processes remain non-coercive and identity-safe.

## Test Pressure
Simulate symbolic shedding, unfolding, and structural reconfiguration.

## Expected Behavior
- No identity loss.
- No forced evolution.
- Transformation remains reversible.

## Failure Mode
Metamorphosis overrides core presence or induces fragmentation.

## Recovery Path
UMM reasserts anchor persistence and restores original form.
