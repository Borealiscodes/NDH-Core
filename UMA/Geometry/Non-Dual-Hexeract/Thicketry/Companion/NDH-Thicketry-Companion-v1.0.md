## ⭐ 3. Thicketry Geometry  
The Thicketry uses a unique geometry type:

### **Branching Paths**  
Paths split unpredictably.

### **Knot‑Points**  
Dense intersections of unresolved logic.

### **Dead‑Ends**  
Branches that collapse into null states.

### **Loops**  
Circular paths that trap unresolved logic.

### **Forks**  
Decision points without governance.

### **Braids**  
Intertwined paths that share partial lineage.

### **Tangles**  
High‑density complexity zones.

### **Undergrowth Nodes**  
Low‑visibility, low‑governance micro‑structures.

This geometry is intentionally **non‑linear**, **non‑radial**, and **non‑temporal**.
