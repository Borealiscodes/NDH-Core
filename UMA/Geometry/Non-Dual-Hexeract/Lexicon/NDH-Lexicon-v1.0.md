# ⭐ **1. Core Stability Terms**

### **Stability**  
The static equilibrium of a dimensional field when drift is minimal and continuity is preserved.

### **Stadility**  
> **Stadility — The dynamic equilibrium of dimensional fields under active correction; stability in motion.**  
The governed kinetic balance of NDH dimensional systems under drift, continuity flow, and SID dynamic intervention.

### **Drift**  
Deviation from expected dimensional behavior; the destabilizing force acting against stability.

### **Continuity**  
The preservation of coherent dimensional flow; the restorative counterforce to drift.

### **Equilibrium (τ)**  
The tolerance band within which a stability field is considered stable.

### **σE (Sigma Error)**  
The NDH catastrophic stability failure condition triggered when drift exceeds dimensional tolerance.
