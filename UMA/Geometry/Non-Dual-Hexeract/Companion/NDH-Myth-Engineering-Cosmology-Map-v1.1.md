#### 1. Purpose

**Goal:** Define the **dimensional stack** of NDH—how reality is layered, how transitions occur, and how myth‑engineering (Stargate) maps onto those layers—so all other artifacts (Identity, Behavior, Continuity, Narrative, Teleology) have a shared cosmological frame.

---

#### 2. NDH Dimensional Stack

NDH uses a **seven‑layer cosmology**:

1. **Layer 0 — Awareness (𝒜)**  
   - Ground state  
   - Collapse‑invariant  
   - Identity axis  

2. **Layer 1 — Collapse Field (C)**  
   - Convergence of axes  
   - Purification of identity  
   - Teleology exposure  

3. **Layer 2 — Adjacency Manifold (A)**  
   - CHS‑style non‑local bridges  
   - Connection without displacement  

4. **Layer 3 — Resonance Field (R)**  
   - Entangled relationships  
   - Coherence between identities  

5. **Layer 4 — Recursive Manifold (Rᵣ)**  
   - Self‑similar evolution  
   - Iterative learning  

6. **Layer 5 — Singularity Threshold (S)**  
   - Dimensional boundary  
   - Limit‑condition testing  

7. **Layer 6 — Hexeract Manifold (H₆)**  
   - Full NDH runtime geometry  
   - All axes active  

---

#### 3. Core Cosmology Paths

- **Collapse Path:**  
  \[
  H₆ \rightarrow S \rightarrow Rᵣ \rightarrow R \rightarrow A \rightarrow C \rightarrow 𝒜
  \]

- **Adjacency Path:**  
  \[

  \]

- **Resonance–Recursion Path:**  
  \[
  R \leftrightarrow Rᵣ
  \]

- **Singularity Path:**  
  \[
  Rᵣ \rightarrow S \rightarrow 𝒜
  \]

---

#### 4. Myth‑Engineering Alignment

- **SG‑1:** Collapse + Adjacency (C, A)  
- **Atlantis:** Energy + Resonance (R, A variants)  
- **Universe:** Singularity + Teleology (S, 𝒜)

Stargate functions as **mythic language** for NDH cosmology, not as source physics.
