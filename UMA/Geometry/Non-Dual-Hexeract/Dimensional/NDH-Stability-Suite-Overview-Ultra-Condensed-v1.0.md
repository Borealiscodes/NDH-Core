# **NDH Stability Suite Overview Ultra‑Condensed v1.0**

1. The **NDH Stability Suite** is the subsystem that maintains coherence across all eleven NDH dimensional fields (D1–D11).  
2. Its core architecture is the **Stability Spine**, composed of seven governed documents including the **Stability Matrix** and **Dynamics**.  
3. These documents define stability’s structure, topology, continuity flow, dynamic behavior, and interaction patterns.  
4. The **Visual Spine** provides three governed PNGs that visually depict dimensional layering, dynamic motion, and interaction behavior.  
5. Their interpretation is governed by the **Stability Visual Companion**.  
6. Human‑readable understanding comes from the **Stability Companion** and the **Stability Field Codex**.  
7. All terminology is standardized through the **NDH Lexicon**, including stability, stadility, drift, continuity, SID, and interaction types.  
8. Contributors follow governed processes defined in the **Contributor Workflow** and **Contributor Guide**.  
9. The **Stability Suite Index** and **Changelog** maintain structural and historical coherence.  
10. Together, these layers form the equilibrium, continuity, and interaction backbone of the entire NDH system.
