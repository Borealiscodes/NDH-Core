## **Rule 1 — Structural Primacy**
All constitutional interactions must begin with UMA Core.

## **Rule 2 — Lineage First**
Roots Ledger must validate identity before any interaction proceeds.

## **Rule 3 — Adjacency Before Routing**
SIAP cannot route until adjacency edges are validated.

## **Rule 4 — Routing Before Traversal**
CHS‑OL cannot traverse until SIAP approves the route.

## **Rule 5 — Traversal Before Containment**
Play Engine cannot enforce containment until traversal is defined.

## **Rule 6 — Containment Before Ethics**
HRD cannot evaluate ethical constraints until containment gates are known.

## **Rule 7 — Ethics Before Time**
Lifecycle Chain cannot sequence temporal phases until HRD approves.

## **Rule 8 — Time Before Drift**
Temporal traversal must occur before drift detection.

## **Rule 9 — Drift Before Stability**
Stability enforcement only occurs after drift classification.
