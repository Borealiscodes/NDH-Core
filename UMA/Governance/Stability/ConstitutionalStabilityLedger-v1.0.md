# **4. Ledger Event Types**

### **4.1 Stability Confirmation Event**  
Subsystem passes all stability checks.

### **4.2 Soft Instability Event**  
Minor deviation detected; corrected without rollback.

### **4.3 Hard Instability Event**  
Major deviation; rollback or containment lock required.

### **4.4 Drift Event**  
Temporal identity mismatch detected.

### **4.5 Quarantine Event**  
Subsystem isolated due to severe instability.

### **4.6 Reintegration Event**  
Subsystem restored after stability validation.
