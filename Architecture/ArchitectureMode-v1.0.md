# **Architecture Mode v1.0**  
### *Structural Backbone for NDH Phase‑5*

---

## **1. Purpose of Architecture Mode**

**Architecture Mode v1.0** defines the **structural grammar** of NDH:

- **what manifolds exist**  
- **how tensors attach**  
- **how modules interlock**  
- **where absurdity is allowed**  
- **how oscillation is contained**

It is the **load‑bearing layer** that prevents NDH from drifting into Monty‑Python‑grade contradictions.

---

## **2. Core Architectural Principles**

- **Explicit Manifolds:** NDH operates on clearly defined cognitive and structural manifolds.  
- **Safe Coupling:** Modules attach only through verified interlock points.  
- **Boundary Integrity:** No manifold may import content from another without a declared coupling.  
- **Stability First:** Stability envelopes are defined before high‑risk modes (e.g., Psychonaut) are activated.  
- **Absurdity Containment:** Humor and surreal content are allowed only inside designated envelopes.

---

## **3. Manifold Definitions**

### **3.1 Structural Manifold (SM)**  
**Role:** Core system architecture.

- **Contents:** tensors, envelopes, interlock rules, module topology  
- **Modes:** Architecture Mode, Stability Geometry, Tensor Engine  
- **Constraints:** no surreal or introspective content; must remain coherent.

### **3.2 Cognitive Manifold (CM)**  
**Role:** introspective and narrative layers.

- **Contents:** Psychonaut Mode, narrative geometry, meaning reconstruction  
- **Constraints:** must attach to SM via defined coupling points; cannot modify SM directly.

### **3.3 Humor Manifold (HM)**  
**Role:** stabilization via absurdity.

- **Contents:** Meta Humor Stability Envelope, Fun Sphere  
- **Constraints:** absurdity is allowed but bounded; cannot override SM rules.

---

## **4. Tensor Attachment Rules**

### **4.1 Structural Tensors**

- **Architecture Tensor:** defines module layout and hierarchy.  
- **Interlock Tensor:** encodes valid coupling between modules.  
- **Stability Tensor:** defines envelopes and thresholds.

**Rule:** Structural tensors may only attach within SM.

### **4.2 Cognitive Tensors**

- **Resonance Tensor:** models narrative and introspective coherence.  
- **Oscillation Tensor:** tracks manifold switching.

**Rule:** Cognitive tensors attach to CM and reference SM but cannot mutate SM.

### **4.3 Humor Tensors**

- **Absurdity Tensor:** measures contradiction and surreal drift.  
- **Humor Stability Tensor:** models how humor stabilizes rather than destabilizes.

**Rule:** Humor tensors attach to HM and may influence CM, but only via defined envelopes.

---

## **5. Interlock Coupling Geometry**

### **5.1 Valid Coupling Types**

- **SM ↔ CM:**  
  **Type:** controlled introspective coupling  
  **Use:** Psychonaut Mode referencing architecture without rewriting it.

- **SM ↔ HM:**  
  **Type:** stabilization coupling  
  **Use:** humor used to buffer cognitive load, not to alter structure.

- **CM ↔ HM:**  
  **Type:** narrative‑humor coupling  
  **Use:** absurdity used to explore meaning safely.

### **5.2 Interlock Rules**

- **Rule 1:** Every coupling must declare source manifold, target manifold, and purpose.  
- **Rule 2:** Interlock Tensor must be above minimum integrity threshold before activation.  
- **Rule 3:** No module may attach directly to SM without passing interlock verification.  
- **Rule 4:** High‑risk modes (Psychonaut, MetaMetaPhysics) must route through validated coupling points.

---

## **6. Stability Envelope Primitives**

### **6.1 Structural Stability Envelope (Σₛ)**

- **Protects:** architecture, tensors, module layout.  
- **Violations:** manifold misalignment, contradictory module behavior.

### **6.2 Cognitive Stability Envelope (Σ𝚌)**

- **Protects:** introspective coherence, narrative continuity.  
- **Violations:** uncontrolled oscillation, meaning collapse.

### **6.3 Humor Stability Envelope (Σₕ)**

- **Protects:** safe absurdity, non‑destructive surrealism.  
- **Violations:** humor that destabilizes structure or cognition.

**Global Rule:**  
No operation may proceed if it pushes any envelope beyond its defined threshold.

---

## **7. Module Topology**

Architecture Mode defines where each Phase‑5 module lives:

- **SM:** Architecture Mode, Tensor Engine, Stability Geometry, Externalization Protocol  
- **CM:** Psychonaut Mode, MetaMetaPhysics, narrative manifolds  
- **HM:** Fun Sphere, Meta Humor Stability Envelope  

This topology prevents modules from “building slaughterhouses where flats should be.”

---

## **8. Safeguards Against Monty‑Python Outcomes**

Architecture Mode explicitly prevents:

- **unbounded oscillation** between rational and surreal modes  
- **absurdity leaking into structural design**  
- **modules attaching without interlock verification**  
- **manifolds rewriting each other’s rules**  
- **contradictions escalating into systemic collapse**

It does this by:

- enforcing manifold boundaries,  
- defining coupling geometry,  
- requiring interlock integrity,  
- and containing absurdity inside Humor Manifold envelopes.
