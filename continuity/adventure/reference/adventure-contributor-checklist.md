# 📄 **Adventure Contributor Checklist**  
### Continuity Adventure System — Contributor Workflow  
A calm, structured checklist for contributors creating adventures in the Continuity Repo.

---

## 🌲 1. Purpose  
This checklist provides a **step‑by‑step workflow** for contributors who want to create adventures, nodes, scenes, or expansions within the Continuity Adventure System.

It is designed to be:

- gentle  
- reversible  
- ND‑paced  
- non‑coercive  
- easy to follow  

---

# ✅ **Contributor Checklist**

---

## 1. **Choose Your Contribution Type**  
Start by identifying what you want to create.

- **World Node**  
- **Character Node**  
- **Story Node**  
- **Adventure Map**  
- **Symbolic System**  
- **Random Adventure**  

---

## 2. **Write Your Fragments**  
Provide any fragments you want the system to use.

Examples:

- world detail  
- character trait  
- soft quest  
- mood  
- symbolic echo  

Guided Link: **Provide Story Elements**

---

## 3. **Check ND‑Pacing Requirements**  
Ensure your contribution is:

- gentle  
- slow  
- breathable  
- reversible  
- non‑coercive  
- symbolic (hazards only)  

Guided Link: **Review ND‑Pacing**

---

## 4. **Build the Node**  
Use the correct structure:

### World Node  
- biome  
- stability membrane  
- symbolic hazards  
- stillness nodes  

### Character Node  
- intent  
- tone  
- ability  
- ecology link  
- reversible traits  
- safety header  

### Story Node  
- soft quest  
- reversible scene  
- pacing membrane  
- gentle arc  
- stillness checkpoint  

Guided Link: **Node Templates**

---

## 5. **Integrate Into the Adventure Loop**  
Ensure your node fits into:

```
World → Character → Story
```

Guided Link: **Adventure Loop**

---

## 6. **Add Optional Systems (If Relevant)**  
Optional symbolic systems:

- Echo Tokens  
- Resonance Map  
- Stillness Sanctuaries  

Guided Link: **Optional Systems**

---

## 7. **Place Files Correctly**  
Use the correct repo directories:

- `continuity/adventure/`  
- `continuity/adventure/maps/`  
- `continuity/adventure/characters/`  
- `continuity/adventure/reference/`  

Guided Link: **File Placement Guide**

---

## 8. **Check Continuity Safety**  
Before finalizing, confirm:

- no collapse engines  
- no forced outcomes  
- no metaphysical triggers  
- no irreversible states  
- no destabilizing logic  

Guided Link: **Continuity Safety**

---

## 9. **Document Your Contribution**  
Add a small note describing:

- what you added  
- why you added it  
- how it fits the adventure system  

Guided Link: **Contributor Notes**
