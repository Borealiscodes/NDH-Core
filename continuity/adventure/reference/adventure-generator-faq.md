# 📄 **Adventure Generator FAQ**  
### Continuity Adventure System — Forest of Echoes Edition  
Frequently asked questions about generating adventures from story fragments.

---

## 🌲 1. What is the Adventure Generator?  
The Adventure Generator is a system that takes **small story fragments** and produces a **full multi‑mode adventure**.

It builds:

- a **World Node**  
- a **Character Node**  
- a **Story Node**  
- a **reversible adventure loop**  
- a **four‑chapter progression**  
- optional symbolic systems  

Guided Link: **Adventure Generator README**

---

## 🌿 2. What counts as a “story fragment”?  
Anything that feels like part of a world, character, or moment.

Examples:

- “a soft hum behind a tree”  
- “a wanderer who hesitates before stepping forward”  
- “a pool that reflects sound instead of light”  
- “slow, eerie mood”  
- “an echo shaped like a memory”  

Guided Link: **Provide Story Elements**

---

## 🌳 3. How many fragments do I need?  
Just **one**.

The generator can build a full adventure from a single fragment.  
More fragments simply add more texture.

## 🔮 5. Is the generator deterministic?  
**Structure:** deterministic  
**Content:** non‑deterministic  
**Safety:** guaranteed  
**Reversibility:** guaranteed  

This means:

- the adventure always has the same shape  
- the details vary based on fragments  
- pacing and safety rules never change
