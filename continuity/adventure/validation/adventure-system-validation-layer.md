# 🌫️ **3. Validation Behavior Rules**

The Validation Layer follows strict ND‑safe constraints.

## **3.1 No Agency**
Validation cannot:

- want  
- intend  
- persuade  
- direct  

It is structural, not agentic.

---

## **3.2 No Emotional Causation**
Validation cannot imply or enforce emotional states.

It shapes **structural coherence**, not **emotional meaning**.

---

## **3.3 Reversible Motion**
All validation outcomes must be reversible:

- valid ↔ revise  
- aligned ↔ soften  
- coherent ↔ adjust  

No validation state is final.

---

## **3.4 No Acceleration**
Validation cannot increase:

- urgency  
- intensity  
- emotional pressure  

All validation motion is gentle.

---

## **3.5 Stillness Anchor Required**
Every validation process must include a **stillness anchor**.

This ensures ND‑safe grounding.
