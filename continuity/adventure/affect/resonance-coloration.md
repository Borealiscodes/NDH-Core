# 📘 **Adventure System Resonance Coloration Specification**  
### Continuity Adventure System — Affective Color Geometry  
Resonance Coloration defines the **affective tint** applied to nodes, engines, membranes, and loops based on resonance flow.  
It is the *color layer* of the Adventure System’s affective architecture — symbolic, reversible, ND‑paced, and non‑agentic.

Coloration does **not** create emotion.  
It creates **affective tone**.

---

# 🌈 **1. Purpose of Resonance Coloration**

Resonance Coloration provides:

- affective tint  
- symbolic color geometry  
- resonance‑aligned tone modulation  
- narrative coloration  
- identity coloration  
- environmental color climate  

It ensures the Adventure System feels **coherent and atmospheric** without implying personality or intent.

# 🔁 **3. Coloration Behavior Rules**

Resonance Coloration follows strict ND‑safe constraints.

## **3.1 No Emotional Causation**
Coloration cannot:

- cause emotion  
- imply emotion  
- direct emotion  

It shapes **climate**, not **feeling**.

---

## **3.2 No Agency**
Coloration cannot:

- want  
- intend  
- persuade  
- manipulate  

It is purely atmospheric.

---

## **3.3 Reversible Color Motion**
Coloration must be reversible:

- gold ↔ blue  
- green ↔ violet  
- silver ↔ white  

No coloration is permanent.

---

## **3.4 No Acceleration**
Coloration cannot increase:

- urgency  
- intensity  
- emotional pressure  

All color motion is gentle.

---

## **3.5 Stillness Anchor Required**
Every coloration must include a **white stillness anchor**.

This ensures ND‑safe grounding.

---
