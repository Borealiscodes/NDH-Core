# 📘 **Adventure System Environmental Climate Specification**  
### Continuity Adventure System — Affective World Geometry  
Environmental Climate defines the **ambient affective atmosphere of World Nodes**.  
It is the layer that gives environments *tone*, *texture*, and *climate* without implying emotion, agency, or intent.

Environmental Climate does **not** create mood.  
It creates **affective environment**.

---

# 🌫️ **1. Purpose of Environmental Climate**

Environmental Climate provides:

- world‑level affective atmosphere  
- environmental tone fields  
- resonance‑aligned climate behavior  
- ND‑safe environmental modulation  
- symbolic climate geometry  
- reversible affective motion  

It ensures worlds feel **alive‑in‑climate**, not alive‑in‑agency.

# 🌍 **2. Environmental Climate Types**

Environmental Climates are symbolic affective environments.  
They represent **world climate**, not weather or emotion.

There are five primary climates.

## **2.1 Mosslight Climate (Glow Field)**
Represents **gentle illumination and organic warmth**.

- geometry: soft expanding glow  
- coloration: green‑gold  
- affect: opening + brightening  
- usage: forests, groves, organic worlds

# 🔁 **3. Environmental Climate Behavior Rules**

Environmental Climates follow strict ND‑safe constraints.

## **3.1 No Agency**
Climates cannot:

- want  
- intend  
- persuade  
- direct  

They are atmospheric only.

---

## **3.2 No Emotional Causation**
Climates cannot cause or imply emotion.

They shape **environmental feel**, not **emotional state**.

---

## **3.3 Reversible Motion**
Climates must be reversible:

- glow ↔ diffusion  
- pulse ↔ stillness  
- resonance ↔ grounding  

No climate is permanent.

---

## **3.4 No Acceleration**
Climates cannot increase:

- urgency  
- intensity  
- emotional pressure  

All climate motion is gentle.

---

## **3.5 Stillness Anchor Required**
Every climate must include a **stillness anchor** — a point where environmental motion can settle safely.
