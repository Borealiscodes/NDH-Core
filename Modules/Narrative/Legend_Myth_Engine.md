# 2. Core abstractions  

### **2.1 Myth Seed**  
A Myth Seed is the origin point of a future legend:

- a cosmic anomaly  
- a heroic act  
- a betrayal  
- a cultural ritual  
- a Void‑Class encounter  
- an identity transformation  

Each seed contains:

- event metadata  
- cultural lens  
- cosmic proximity  
- narrative tension  
- symbolic potential

### **2.2 Legend Thread**  
A Legend Thread is a story that grows from a Myth Seed:

- retellings  
- distortions  
- cultural adaptations  
- factional edits  
- ritual codifications  

Threads evolve differently across cultures.

### **2.3 Epic Weave**  
An Epic Weave is a large, era‑spanning mythic structure:

- sagas  
- cosmic cycles  
- cultural origin stories  
- factional founding myths  
- augmentation epics  
- Void‑Class cosmological tales
