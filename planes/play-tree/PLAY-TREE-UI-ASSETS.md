# 🌳✨ **PLAY-TREE-UI-ASSETS.md**  
### *Glyphs, Icons, Shapes, and Soft Visual Assets for the Play Tree Plane*

```
# ✨ PLAY TREE — UI ASSETS ✨
### Glyphs, Icons, Shapes, and Soft Visual Assets

## ❗ Play Tree Boundary Rule — No Mixing with Atropus
The Play Tree is a warm, expressive, ND-safe plane. It must never mix with
Atropus, reference Atropus, or incorporate null-plane tone, logic, or
commentary. The Play Tree and Atropus operate on entirely different tonal,
structural, and constitutional layers. They remain permanently separate.

This document defines the visual assets used in the Play Tree UI. All assets
must be soft, warm, reversible, ND-safe, and consistent with the tonal and
structural principles of the Play Tree plane.
```

---

# 🌳 **1. Asset Philosophy**
Play Tree assets must be:

- soft  
- warm  
- low-contrast  
- reversible  
- non-symbolic  
- non-emotional  
- ND-safe  

They must **never**:

- flash  
- jitter  
- imply urgency  
- imply evaluation  
- resemble Atropus glyphs  

---

# 🌳 **2. Asset Categories**
The Play Tree uses five categories of assets:

1. **Root Glyphs**  
2. **Branch Icons**  
3. **Leaf Shapes**  
4. **Sap Flow Indicators**  
5. **Weather Overlays**

Each category corresponds to a structural layer.

---

# 🌳 **3. Root Glyphs**
Root glyphs represent grounding and stability.

### **3.1 Primary Root Glyph**
- soft trunk silhouette  
- rounded edges  
- warm brown tone  
- slow pulse animation  

### **3.2 Reset Glyph**
- circular trunk ring  
- soft fade animation  
- reversible motion  

---

# 🌳 **4. Branch Icons**
Branch icons represent play modes.

### **4.1 Exploration Icon**
- branching lines  
- soft green tone  
- gentle outward motion  

### **4.2 Calibration Icon**
- balanced branch pair  
- muted gold tone  
- slow symmetrical pulse  

### **4.3 Reflection Icon**
- inward-curving branches  
- soft blue tone  
- slow inward drift  

### **4.4 Stillness Icon**
- minimal branch shape  
- warm grey tone  
- no motion  

---

# 🌳 **5. Leaf Shapes**
Leaf shapes represent micro-interactions.

### **5.1 Standard Leaf**
- rounded teardrop shape  
- soft green tone  
- tap-light animation  

### **5.2 Expanded Leaf**
- slightly larger shape  
- muted tone  
- slow drift motion  

### **5.3 Dimmed Leaf**
- low-contrast shape  
- reduced density  
- no motion  

---

# 🌳 **6. Sap Flow Indicators**
Sap Flow indicators represent tonal gradients.

### **6.1 Warmth Indicator**
- soft gold gradient  
- slow upward drift  

### **6.2 Coolness Indicator**
- muted blue gradient  
- slow downward drift  

### **6.3 Intensity Indicator**
- gentle pulse  
- reversible amplitude  

---

# 🌳 **7. Weather Overlays**
Weather overlays represent environmental modulation.

### **7.1 Cloud Overlay**
- soft white shapes  
- slow horizontal drift  
- low opacity  

### **7.2 Breeze Lines**
- thin curved lines  
- slow wave motion  
- muted tone  

### **7.3 Soft Light Overlay**
- warm gradient wash  
- slow fade-in/out  

---

# 🌳 **8. Asset Motion Rules**
All motion must be:

- slow  
- gentle  
- reversible  
- predictable  

Forbidden motion:

- snapping  
- jitter  
- flashing  
- sudden acceleration  

---

# 🌳 **9. Asset Color Rules**
Approved tones:

- soft greens  
- warm browns  
- muted blues  
- gentle golds  
- warm greys  

Forbidden tones:

- harsh red  
- neon  
- high-contrast black  
- flashing colors  

---

# 🌳 **10. Asset Safety**
Assets must:

- avoid sensory overload  
- avoid symbolic meaning  
- avoid emotional implication  
- avoid coercion  
- maintain warmth
