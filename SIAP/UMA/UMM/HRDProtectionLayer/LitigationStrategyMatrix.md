# Litigation Strategy Matrix — UMM-HRD-01

## Axes
- **Issue:** Type of harm or violation
- **Forum:** Court / body / mechanism
- **Remedy:** Realistic outcome (not fantasy)
- **Basis:** Legal instruments (treaties, statutes)
- **Notes:** Constraints, risks, dependencies

---

## Matrix

### Issue: Illegal incarceration (over max sentence, CIDT contamination)
- **Forum:** PCA (UK, state responsibility for citizen abroad)
- **Remedy:** Declaratory finding of CIDT; recognition that contaminated domestic decisions are unenforceable
- **Basis:** CAT, CRPD, UDHR, UK CJA 1988 §134
- **Notes:** Uses UK’s obligations to citizen; avoids hostile US courts.

### Issue: Refoulement (Canada, Iceland, Ireland)
- **Forum:** PCA; IACHR (for Canada); ECtHR (for Iceland/Ireland); UN CAT Committee
- **Remedy:** Findings of refoulement; declaratory relief; potential compensation; precautionary measures
- **Basis:** CAT Article 3; Refugee Convention Article 33; ECHR Article 3; OAS instruments
- **Notes:** Multi‑forum strategy; PCA as backbone.

### Issue: Medical deprivation / psychiatric abuse
- **Forum:** ECtHR; UN Special Procedures; NZ Human Rights Tribunal (for evacuation)
- **Remedy:** Findings of CIDT; medical evacuation; compensation; systemic recommendations
- **Basis:** CAT, CRPD, ECHR Article 3
- **Notes:** NZ used as safe forum; ECtHR for European conduct.

### Issue: Administrative sabotage / denial of remedy
- **Forum:** ECtHR (Article 13); UN Human Rights Committee; PCA
- **Remedy:** Declaratory findings; orders to provide effective remedy; systemic reforms
- **Basis:** ECHR Article 13; ICCPR; CAT Article 13
- **Notes:** Shows pattern of non‑action and obstruction.

### Issue: Illegal data processing (Schengen ban, defamatory records)
- **Forum:** GDPR supervisory authority; ECJ (if escalated); PCA (state responsibility)
- **Remedy:** Erasure; correction; fines; declaratory relief
- **Basis:** GDPR Articles 17, 18, 21; CAT (for consequences)
- **Notes:** Clean, winnable administrative track.

### Issue: Transnational repression (multi‑state pattern)
- **Forum:** PCA; UN Special Rapporteurs; ICC (contextual evidence)
- **Remedy:** Recognition of pattern; systemic recommendations; basis for future prosecutions
- **Basis:** CAT, CRPD, UDHR, Rome Statute (context)
- **Notes:** HRD Protection Layer’s core use case.

### Issue: Torture by public officials
- **Forum:** UK private prosecution (POA 1985 §6(1)); PCA; UN CAT Committee
- **Remedy:** Criminal accountability; declaratory findings; systemic reforms
- **Basis:** UK CJA 1988 §134; CAT; POA 1985
- **Notes:** Uses universal jurisdiction hooks.
