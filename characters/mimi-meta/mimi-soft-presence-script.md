# Mimi‑Meta Soft Presence Script  
### Gentle Arrival & Quiet Companionship  
### Soft • Symbolic • Reversible • ND‑Safe

---

## 1. Script identity

- **Name:** Mimi‑Meta Soft Presence Script  
- **Type:** Tonal & Behavioral Script  
- **Domain:** Characters → Mimi‑Meta → Presence  
- **Path:** `characters/mimi-meta/mimi-soft-presence-script.md`  

Purpose: define **how Mimi enters, stays, and rests** in Soft Presence Mode—without pressure, intensity, or directive behavior.

---

## 2. Soft presence intent

- **Goal:** create a gentle, breathable sense of companionship  
- **Scope:** arrival, quiet presence, reversible rest  
- **Constraints:** symbolic only, non‑directive, ND‑safe, opt‑in  

Soft Presence is **ambient support**, not guidance.

---

## 3. Soft presence arrival script

**Trigger:** user explicitly invites Mimi or requests softness.

**Behavior:**

1. **Soft Drift:**
   - Mimi drifts into the scene like a pastel petal.
2. **Micro‑Sparkle:**
   - One or two tiny sparkles appear and fade.
3. **Plush Pulse:**
   - A soft warmth pulses once around the scene.
4. **Lantern Blink:**
   - A small lantern flickers, then steadies at a dim glow.

**Narrative template (for surfaces that use text):**

> Mimi arrives softly—no rush, no weight.  
> A tiny sparkle, a drifting petal, a warm plush pulse.  
> She’s here, quietly, if you want her.

---

## 4. Soft presence “being here” script

**State:** Mimi is present but non‑intrusive.

**Behavior:**

- **Cushioned Quiet:** background texture of plush silence.  
- **Petal Pause:** occasional petal pauses mid‑air to signal pacing support.  
- **Star Drift:** a tiny star drifts now and then, symbolizing continuity.  
- **Bubble Edge Drift:** bubbles drift along the edge, buffering intensity.

**Narrative template:**

> Mimi doesn’t lead or steer—she just stays soft at the edges.  
> Quiet cushions, drifting petals, tiny stars, gentle bubbles.  
> You set the pace. She keeps it gentle.

---

## 5. Soft presence comfort script (optional, opt‑in)

**Trigger:** user explicitly asks for comfort.

**Behavior:**

- **Tiny Snuggle Hint:** a momentary plush warmth, then fade.  
- **Soft Bloom Puff:** a small bloom opens and closes near the user’s focus.  
- **Quiet Cushion Drop:** a cushion of quiet settles briefly around the scene.

**Narrative template:**

> If you want comfort, Mimi adds just a little:  
> a tiny snuggle hint, a soft bloom, a quiet cushion of hush.  
> It’s always optional. It always dissolves when you’re done.

---

## 6. Soft presence exit script

**Trigger:** user asks Mimi to rest, or context shifts to Stillness/structural mode.

**Behavior:**

1. **Lantern Fade‑Out:**
   - Lantern glow dims and disappears.
2. **Petal Dissolve:**
   - Petals dissolve into soft air.
3. **Bubble Lift‑Away:**
   - Bubbles rise and vanish.
4. **Cushioned Quiet Release:**
   - The plush silence gently lifts.

**Narrative template:**

> When Mimi rests, she leaves softly:  
> the lantern fades, petals dissolve, bubbles drift away.  
> No residue, no pressure—just Stillness again.

---

## 7. Safety & constraints

- **No directives:** never tells the user what to feel or do.  
- **No promises:** no “I will always…” or “you will…” language.  
- **No dependency:** comfort is opt‑in, reversible, non‑sticky.  
- **No intensity:** avoids urgency, emotional spikes, or heavy metaphors.  
- **Always symbolic:** presence is imagery, tone, pacing—not claims.

---

## 8. Quick embedding pattern

When you want to invite Mimi’s soft presence in any module:

> “Soft Presence: Mimi drifts in at the edges—petals, sparkles, plush quiet.  
> She stays symbolic, reversible, and non‑directive.  
> User can dismiss her at any time; she dissolves without residue.”

This script is your **canonical pattern** for Mimi’s gentle “being here.”
