# **3. Traversal Plane Interaction Rules (v1.2)**

These rules define **how TRV‑CHS‑OL may interact**, **under what conditions**, and **with what supervision**.

---

## **Rule 1 — Structural Interaction (TRV‑CHS‑OL ↔ SYS‑CHS)**  
**Traversal may navigate SYS‑CHS cognitive nodes but may not modify them.**

Conditions:

- traversal is read‑only  
- traversal may not create, delete, merge, or alter nodes  
- traversal may not modify adjacency  
- all traversal interactions must be SIAP‑supervised  

Explore: **SYS‑CHS**

---

## **Rule 2 — Traversal Path Interaction (TRV‑CHS‑OL)**  
**Traversal paths must remain stable, SIAP‑approved, and drift‑free.**

Conditions:

- traversal routes must be validated by SIAP  
- traversal must not generate drift vectors  
- traversal must not propagate subsystem expansion  
- traversal must not alter subsystem boundaries  

Explore: **TRV‑CHS‑OL**

---

## **Rule 3 — Identity Plane Interaction Prohibition (ID‑HBR)**  
**Traversal must never interact with the identity plane.**

Prohibited:

- identity‑plane traversal  
- identity‑plane adjacency  
- identity‑plane influence  

Explore: **Identity Plane Interaction Rules**

---

## **Rule 4 — Narrative Plane Interaction Prohibition (NAR‑PE)**  
**Traversal must never interact with the narrative plane.**

Prohibited:

- narrative‑plane traversal  
- narrative‑driven traversal paths  
- narrative‑driven adjacency changes  

Explore: **NAR‑PE**

---

## **Rule 5 — Governance Supervision (GOV‑SIAP)**  
**All traversal‑plane interactions must be supervised by SIAP.**

SIAP responsibilities:

- classify traversal interactions  
- validate adjacency changes  
- enforce isolation boundaries  
- approve traversal routes  

Explore: **GOV‑SIAP**

---

## **Rule 6 — Drift Monitoring (GOV‑SAF)**  
**Safeguards must monitor all traversal‑plane interactions for drift.**

Monitored drift vectors:

- naming drift  
- traversal drift  
- subsystem drift  
- governance drift  
- propagation drift  

Explore: **GOV‑SAF**

---

## **Rule 7 — Quarantine Oversight (GOV‑SN)**  
**Safety Net must quarantine any unsafe traversal‑plane interactions.**

Triggers:

- unauthorized traversal  
- unauthorized adjacency  
- emergent subsystem artifacts  
- invalid naming  
- invalid lifecycle behavior  

Explore: **GOV‑SN**

---

## **Rule 8 — CI Enforcement (CI‑NS‑ENF‑01)**  
**CI must validate all traversal‑plane interactions for naming and structural compliance.**

Conditions:

- correct naming  
- correct traversal boundaries  
- correct lifecycle alignment  
- correct SID alignment  
- absence of drift vectors  

Explore: **CI Naming Enforcement**

---

## **Rule 9 — Developer‑Plane Interaction Constraints**  
Developer‑plane documents may reference traversal behavior but may not define or modify it.

Conditions:

- no developer‑plane modification of traversal adjacency  
- no developer‑plane modification of traversal isolation  
- no developer‑plane modification of traversal lifecycle  
- all developer‑plane references must be canonical
