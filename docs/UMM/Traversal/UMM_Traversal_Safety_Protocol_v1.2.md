# **3. Traversal Safety Rules (v1.2)**

These rules define how traversal may occur, under what conditions, and with what supervision.

---

## **Rule 1 — SIAP‑Approved Traversal Only**  
**All traversal routes must be approved by SIAP before activation.**

Conditions:

- SIAP validates traversal paths  
- SIAP ensures adjacency stability  
- SIAP prevents cross‑plane contamination  

Explore: **GOV‑SIAP**

---

## **Rule 2 — Cognitive Node Integrity (SYS‑CHS)**  
**Traversal may read cognitive nodes but may not modify them.**

Prohibited actions:

- node creation  
- node deletion  
- node merging  
- adjacency modification  

Explore: **SYS‑CHS**

---

## **Rule 3 — Traversal Path Stability (TRV‑CHS‑OL)**  
**Traversal paths must remain stable and drift‑free.**

Requirements:

- no traversal‑driven drift  
- no traversal‑driven propagation vectors  
- no traversal‑driven subsystem expansion  

Explore: **TRV‑CHS‑OL**
