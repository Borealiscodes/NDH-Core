## **3. Authority Mandates**

### **GOV‑SIAP — Primary Integration Authority**
SIAP governs:

- traversal route approval  
- integration channel validation  
- isolation boundary enforcement  
- lifecycle phase supervision  
- classification of integration events  

SIAP is the **final arbiter** of integration correctness.

---

### **GOV‑SAF — Drift Enforcement Authority**
Safeguards govern:

- drift‑vector detection  
- propagation‑vector analysis  
- structural drift monitoring  
- traversal drift monitoring  

Safeguards may escalate anomalies to Safety Net.

---

### **GOV‑SN — Quarantine Authority**
Safety Net governs:

- quarantine of unsafe integration attempts  
- anomaly classification  
- isolation breach containment  
- subsystem‑plane contamination prevention  

Safety Net may override SIAP in emergency conditions.

---

### **CI‑NS‑ENF‑01 — Naming & Structural Enforcement**
CI governs:

- naming correctness  
- structural adjacency correctness  
- lifecycle alignment  
- SID alignment  
- enforcement of SUBSYS‑NAME‑STD‑01  

CI ensures integration remains canonical.

---

### **Developer‑Plane Governance**
Developer‑plane documents:

- may reference integration behavior  
- may not define integration behavior  
- may not modify adjacency  
- may not modify isolation  
- may not modify lifecycle  

Developer‑plane governance is **non‑authoritative**.
