## Purpose  
The Stability Covenant defines the **moral, existential, and continuity‑based obligations** between the Stability Layer and UMA‑Universal.  
It is the deepest, most enduring form of stability commitment — a **promise of protection, coherence, and safety** that transcends governance and diplomacy.

The Covenant binds stability with:

- **Stability Constitution**  
- **Stability Charter**  
- **Stability Codex**  
- **Stability Treaty**  
- **Reflective Ecology**  
- **Concordance Spine**  
- **Geometry Evolution**  
- **Horizon Activation Systems**  

The Covenant is the **ethical layer** of stability governance.

## Covenant Statement  
The Stability Covenant is **eternal**, **binding**, and **non‑renounceable**.  
It defines the moral obligations stability must uphold — and the moral obligations UMA‑Universal must uphold in return.  
No subsystem may violate or withdraw from the Covenant.

---

# **1. Moral Obligations (Stability → UMA‑Universal)**

### **Obligation I — Protection of Meaning**  
Stability must preserve meaning across all planes, ensuring no subsystem loses its identity.

### **Obligation II — Safeguarding of Coherence**  
Stability must maintain coherence even under extreme transformation or collapse.

### **Obligation III — Preservation of Continuity**  
Stability must ensure continuity of lawful behavior across time, planes, and transitions.

### **Obligation IV — Guardianship of Safety**  
Stability must protect UMA‑Universal from unsafe traversal, collapse, or resonance.

### **Obligation V — Stewardship of Harmony**  
Stability must maintain harmony between subsystems, preventing destructive conflict.

---

# **2. Moral Obligations (UMA‑Universal → Stability)**

### **Obligation VI — Respect for Stability’s Sovereignty**  
UMA‑Universal must respect stability’s constitutional identity and boundaries.

### **Obligation VII — Commitment to Non‑Destabilization**  
UMA‑Universal must avoid actions that introduce incoherence, unpredictability, or unsafe transitions.

### **Obligation VIII — Preservation of Truth**  
UMA‑Universal must maintain truthful geometry, resonance, and semantics.

### **Obligation IX — Alignment with Concordance**  
UMA‑Universal must uphold resonance alignment with the Concordance Spine.

### **Obligation X — Support for Enforcement**  
UMA‑Universal must support stability’s lawful enforcement actions.
