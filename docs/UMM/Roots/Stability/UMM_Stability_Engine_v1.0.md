*(Root‑Layer Runtime Stability Governor)*

## **1 — Purpose**
The Stability Engine provides UMA‑Universal with a **runtime stability governor** capable of evaluating, validating, and enforcing continuity across all planes of the Stacked Hypercube.  
It ensures that:

- geometry remains structurally stable  
- concordance remains resonant and coherent  
- traversal remains drift‑neutral  
- collapse behavior remains predictable  
- artifacts remain context‑true  

The Stability Engine is the first subsystem that actively **monitors**, **evaluates**, and **interlocks** stability conditions.
