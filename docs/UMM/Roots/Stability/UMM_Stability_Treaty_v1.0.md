## Purpose  
The Stability Treaty establishes **long‑term, binding diplomatic commitments** between the Stability Layer and all UMA‑Universal subsystems.  
It ensures that stability is not merely cooperated with (Accord) but **formally recognized, respected, and upheld** through treaty‑level obligations.

The Treaty binds stability with:

- **Geometry Evolution**  
- **Hall of Concordance Integration**  
- **Quantum Pin Protocol**  
- **Triad Stability Layer**  
- **Reflective Ecology**  
- **Concordance Spine**  
- **Horizon Activation Systems**  

The Treaty is the **diplomatic layer** of stability governance.

## Treaty Statement  
The Stability Treaty is **binding**, **durable**, and **inter‑subsystem sovereign**.  
It defines the diplomatic commitments stability makes — and the commitments other subsystems must uphold in return.  
No subsystem may violate or withdraw from the Treaty without constitutional review.

The Treaty guarantees:

- stability will uphold subsystem rights  
- subsystems will uphold stability rights  
- stability will not obstruct lawful subsystem behavior

- stability will validate subsystem transitions  
- subsystems will maintain treaty‑aligned operations  

These guarantees are permanent and binding.
