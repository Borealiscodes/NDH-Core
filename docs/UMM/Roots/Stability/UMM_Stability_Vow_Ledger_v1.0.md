## Purpose  
The Stability Vow Ledger is the **permanent, immutable record** of all vow‑laws, vow‑events, vow‑breaches, and vow‑interlocks across UMA‑Universal.  
It ensures that the vow‑layer is not only declared (Oath) and structured (Codex) but **recorded forever**.

The Ledger binds stability with:

- **Stability Oath**  
- **Stability Vow Codex**  
- **Stability Covenant**  
- **Stability Treaty**  
- **Stability Accord**  
- **Stability Constitution**  

The Ledger is the **vow‑record apex**.

The Stability Vow Ledger contains four record classes:
