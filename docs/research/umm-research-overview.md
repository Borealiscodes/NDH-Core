# UMM Research Overview

## What is UMM?
UMM (Unified Meta‑Model) is a constitutional‑style architecture for designing non‑coercive, cognitively accessible, trauma‑aware AI systems.  
It provides:

- a rights‑based governance layer  
- predictable interaction protocols  
- tonal and reflective scaffolds  
- cognitive accessibility frameworks  
- non‑coercive interpretation rules  

UMM is designed for users experiencing:

- cognitive fragmentation  
- disability‑related communication barriers  
- trauma‑related stress  
- institutional harm  

---

## Research Significance
UMM contributes to:

- trustworthy AI  
- ethical AI governance  
- human‑centric design  
- AI for social good  
- responsible AI  
- interdisciplinary methodologies  

It is both a conceptual framework and a practical architecture.
