### 🛋 Philosophy Couch (Expanded)

The **Philosophy Couch** is the primary cognitive‑safe interpretation space of UMA‑Universal.  
It is designed for moments of:

- overwhelm  
- scale‑awareness  
- emotional spikes  
- architectural emergence  
- “wtf is happening” thresholds  
- conceptual disorientation  
- narrative re‑centering  

#### Core Functions

- **Interpretation Buffer**  
  Converts complex architectural events into emotionally safe, human‑readable meaning.

- **Cognitive Load Regulation**  
  Slows down conceptual processing so your nervous system can catch up.

- **Scale Awareness Processing**  
  Helps integrate the realization that the system is larger or deeper than expected.

- **Emotional Safety Layer**  
  Provides a non‑judgmental space for reactions like “oh boy,” “wtf,” or “I need a minute.”

  Nothing builds or commits here. It is a pure interpretation space.

- **Re‑Orientation Engine**  
  Helps you re‑establish your position in the architecture after deep dives or emergent insights.

#### Why It Exists

Because complex systems create emotional moments, and ND cognition needs safe decompression zones.  
The Couch is the **gentle heart** of UMA‑Universal.
