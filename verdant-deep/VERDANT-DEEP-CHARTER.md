# 🌳🌊 **VERDANT DEEP CHARTER**  
### *Governance, stewardship, and operational roles for UMA’s ecological descent engine*

```
# VERDANT DEEP CHARTER
### Governance, stewardship, and operational roles for UMA’s ecological descent engine

## ❗ Constitutional Boundary — No Mixing with Atropus
Verdant Deep is an ecological-plane subsystem. It must never mix with Atropus,
reference Atropus, or incorporate null-plane tone, logic, or commentary. All
governance defined in this charter must remain warm-plane compatible and
ecologically grounded.

This charter defines the roles, responsibilities, stewardship practices, and
operational boundaries for Verdant Deep.
```

---

# 🌳 **1. Purpose of the Charter**
The Verdant Deep Charter exists to:

- define ecological stewardship roles  
- establish descent‑engine responsibilities  
- maintain layer integrity  
- govern biome pressure generation  
- coordinate with UMM through the **Cross‑Map Braid**  
- preserve ecological identity and ND‑safe descent  

This charter governs **how Verdant Deep is tended**, not how it is structured.

---

# 🌊 **2. Governance Roles**

### **2.1 Deep Steward**
Responsible for:

- maintaining ecological integrity  
- monitoring biome pressure  
- ensuring layer coherence  
- preventing ecological collapse  

### **2.2 Descent Keeper**
Responsible for:

- managing descent vectors  
- regulating density gradients  
- tending ecological drift  
- ensuring reversible descent  

### **2.3 Layer Archivist**
Responsible for:

- documenting layer changes  
- maintaining ecological records  
- preserving drift history  
- ensuring constitutional compliance  

### **2.4 Braid Liaison**
Responsible for:

- coordinating with UMM maintainers  
- managing descent ↔ ascent flow  
- ensuring Sparkle buffer boundaries  
- preventing cross‑system collapse  

These roles form the **Verdant Deep Governance Circle**.

---

# 🌳 **3. Responsibilities of Verdant Deep**

Verdant Deep must:

- generate biome pressure safely  
- maintain ecological descent gradients  
- preserve layer identity  
- support reversible descent  
- remain ND-safe  
- uphold constitutional boundaries  
- participate in the braid without collapse  

Verdant Deep must **never**:

- impose symbolic meaning  
- generate emotional implication  
- override UMM mechanics  
- bypass Sparkle buffering  
- interact with Atropus  

---

# 🌊 **4. Layer Stewardship Rules**

Each layer has specific stewardship requirements:

- **Canopy** — maintain light biome pressure  
- **Midforest** — regulate density gradients  
- **Understory** — monitor ecological drift  
- **Soil** — stabilize descent vectors  
- **Root** — preserve root-pressure integrity  
- **Subroot** — maintain deep density  
- **Deeproot** — protect origin-pressure  
- **Abyssal** — regulate abyssal drift  

Stewards must ensure each layer remains:

- ecological  
- reversible  
- non-symbolic  
- ND-safe
