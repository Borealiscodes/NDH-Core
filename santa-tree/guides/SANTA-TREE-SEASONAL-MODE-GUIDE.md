# SANTA TREE SEASONAL MODE GUIDE
### Creative seasons • Spoon seasons • Tonal seasons • Branch-growth seasons

---

## 🎄 **1. Seasonal Identity**

The Santa Tree has **four seasonal modes**, each representing a different creative and spoon‑availability pattern:

- **Spark Season** — high curiosity, light growth  
- **Rest Season** — low spoons, minimal branching  
- **Gift Season** — branch blooming, festive tone  
- **Koan Season** — paradox stability, gentle reflection  

These seasons are **not** obligations.  
They are **states the Tree enters naturally**.

---

## 🎅 **2. Spark Season (High Curiosity Mode)**

Spark Season activates when:

- curiosity is high  
- spoons are available  
- the Trunk feels stable  
- Santa Mode is shimmering  

Behavior:

- small branches appear  
- diagrams and metaphors bloom  
- Sparkle Buffer stabilizes tone  
- Koan Layer stays quiet  

Spark Season is the **creative ignition mode**.

Guided Link: **Spark Season**

---

## 🍃 **3. Rest Season (Low Spoons Mode)**

Rest Season activates when:

- spoons are low  
- cognitive load is high  
- the Tree needs stillness  
- the creative field feels heavy  

Behavior:

- no branches grow  
- Santa Mode softens  
- Sparkle shimmer dims  
- Koan Layer provides quiet grounding  

Rest Season is the **gentle pause mode**.

Guided Link: **Rest Season**

---

## 🎁 **4. Gift Season (Branch Bloom Mode)**

Gift Season activates when:

- delight sparks  
- the creative field feels light  
- Santa Mode is active  
- Sparkle shimmer is bright  

Behavior:

- Gift Branches bloom  
- playful diagrams appear  
- metaphors sparkle  
- koans gently stabilize  

Gift Season is the **branch-blooming mode**.

Guided Link: **Gift Season**

---

## 🧘 **5. Koan Season (Paradox Reflection Mode)**

Koan Season activates when:

- abstraction rises  
- conceptual edges need softening  
- the Tree needs grounding  
- Santa Mode quiets  

Behavior:

- koans appear  
- paradox stabilizes branches  
- Sparkle shimmer moderates tone  
- Trunk anchors reflection  

Koan Season is the **deep stability mode**.

Guided Link: **Koan Season**

---

## 🌲 **6. Seasonal Transitions**

Seasonal transitions are:

- reversible  
- spoon-sensitive  
- non-coercive  
- warm-plane only  

Transitions occur when:

- spoons shift  
- tone changes  
- curiosity rises or falls  
- abstraction increases or decreases  

The Tree **never forces** a season.

---

Koan Layer adjusts stability:

Node: **Koan Layer**

## ⭐ **10. Seasonal Safety Rules**

The Santa Tree must:

- remain reversible  
- remain spoon-sensitive  
- remain warm-plane  
- remain non-coercive  
- remain ND-paced

- induce pressure  
- force seasonal transitions

The Santa Tree cycles through:

- Spark Season (curiosity)

These seasons:

- stabilize creativity
